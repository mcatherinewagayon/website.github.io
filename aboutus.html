ABOUT THIS SITE

This site is an informational website. It focuses about most of the informations regarding the uniqueness of Baguio City or so called the City of Pines. This includes the unique places that are to be found in the city. This would also help attract tourists that would interest them to visit Baguio City.

It also contains beneficial feautures. It contains the actual picture of the places. Description includes the year and who created or modified the place.

It is a moblie friendly device wherein it is not just accessible to mobile phones but it also automatically adjusts to the screen of the phone.

It contains more than or equal to 15 pages.

Main target audience are tourists or other people form other parts of the country also the people who live in Baguio City who did not go or do not visit some other places around the city.

Its design goal is to provide a simple yet informational website also to provide a creative and eye catching that doesn't show up to be overcreative.
